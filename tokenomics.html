Palladium Tokenomics

Overview

Palladium (PLM) is a cryptocurrency operating on a proof-of-work consensus mechanism with the sha256 algorithm. The block interval is 10 minutes. Every 210,000 blocks mined, which is approximately every 4 years, the halving occurs. The maximum coin supply of PLM is 100,000,000.
